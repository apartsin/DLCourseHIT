Week 4   Part II · Training Infrastructure

Data Pipelines

Instructor lesson plan: lecture (3 h) and practice (2 h).

Learning objectives

  • Build a custom Dataset and DataLoader.
  • Reason about batching, shuffling, and clean splits.
  • Recognize and avoid data leakage.

🎓Lecture · 3 hours

0:00–0:1010 minRecap & objectivesConnect to the previous week; state this week's objectives.
0:10–0:2515 minMotivationModels are only as good as the data pipeline; leakage silently inflates results.
0:25–1:1045 minDataset and DataLoaderThe Dataset interface: __len__ and __getitem__.; DataLoader batching, shuffling, and workers.; Transforms and where they apply.
1:10–1:2010 minBreak
1:20–2:0545 minSplits and leakageTrain/validation/test discipline.; Fitting normalization statistics on the training split only.; Common sources of leakage; the effect of batch size.
2:05–2:3530 minLive demoBuild a custom Dataset and DataLoader, then show a leak inflating accuracy and fix it.
2:35–2:5015 minWrap-up & lab previewRecap the takeaways and brief this week's lab.
2:50–3:0010 minBuffer & questions
Key takeaways.
  • Fit preprocessing on the training split only.
  • Shuffle training data, not validation or test.
  • Batch size trades gradient noise against speed.

💻Practice · 2 hours

The practice class follows the lab's Build / Predict & probe / Explain & defend model.

0:00–0:1010 minSetup & briefConfirm environments run; restate the lab goal and deliverables.
0:10–0:2515 minPredictStudents write predictions before coding. Predict the effect of batch size on loss-curve smoothness and steps per epoch.; Predict what happens to validation accuracy if normalization stats are computed on the full dataset.
0:25–1:1045 minBuild (AI assistant welcome)Implement a custom Dataset and wrap it in a DataLoader with transforms, batching, and shuffling.; Create a clean train, validation, and test split with a fixed seed.
1:10–1:155 minBreak
1:15–1:4025 minProbeRun controlled experiments and compare results against the predictions.
1:40–1:5515 minExplain & defendIntroduce a deliberate data leak, observe the inflated metric, and explain the mechanism and the fix.
1:55–2:005 minWrap & deliverable checkA pipeline notebook with a working DataLoader.; A short batch-size experiment and the leak demonstration with explanation.
Common pitfalls to pre-empt.
  • Fit normalization on the training split only.
  • shuffle=True for training, False for validation and test.
