Week 5   Part II · Training Infrastructure

Loss Functions & Metrics

Instructor lesson plan: lecture (3 h) and practice (2 h).

Learning objectives

  • Choose a task-appropriate loss.
  • Track metrics that reveal real performance.
  • Write a clean train and evaluation loop.

🎓Lecture · 3 hours

0:00–0:1010 minRecap & objectivesConnect to the previous week; state this week's objectives.
0:10–0:2515 minMotivationThe loss defines what the model optimizes; the metric defines what you care about, and they are not the same.
0:25–1:1045 minLoss functionsCross-entropy (logits and class indices), BCE, and MSE.; When each loss applies.; Numerical stability: pass logits, not softmax probabilities.
1:10–1:2010 minBreak
1:20–2:0545 minMetrics and evaluationAccuracy versus precision, recall, and F1.; Why class imbalance breaks accuracy.; The train/eval loop and model.eval().
2:05–2:3530 minLive demoShow a wrong-loss failure (MSE on classification) and a metric that lies under imbalance.
2:35–2:5015 minWrap-up & lab previewRecap the takeaways and brief this week's lab.
2:50–3:0010 minBuffer & questions
Key takeaways.
  • Optimize the right loss and report the right metric.
  • Accuracy hides minority-class failure.
  • Use logits with CrossEntropyLoss.

💻Practice · 2 hours

The practice class follows the lab's Build / Predict & probe / Explain & defend model.

0:00–0:1010 minSetup & briefConfirm environments run; restate the lab goal and deliverables.
0:10–0:2515 minPredictStudents write predictions before coding. Predict what happens if you use MSE for a classification task, before trying it.
0:25–1:1045 minBuild (Claude welcome)Implement a training loop with loss and metric tracking (accuracy and F1 for classification, or MAE and R2 for regression).
1:10–1:155 minBreak
1:15–1:4025 minProbeRun controlled experiments and compare results against the predictions.
1:40–1:5515 minExplain & defendCritique a Claude-written accuracy metric that is wrong under class imbalance; explain the bug, fix it, and report a better metric.
1:55–2:005 minWrap & deliverable checkA train and eval loop with metric logging.; The wrong-loss experiment and the metric critique with fix.
Common pitfalls to pre-empt.
  • CrossEntropyLoss expects logits and class indices, not softmax plus MSE.
  • Accuracy hides minority-class failure; check per-class metrics or F1.
